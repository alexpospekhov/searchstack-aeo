**Tweet 3 (what it does — the visual)**

One command shows you everything:

```
$ searchstack ai
ChatGPT:     3/5 cited ✅
Perplexity:  4/5 cited ✅ (with source URLs)
Claude:      0/5 ❌
Grok:        2/5 ✅

$ searchstack geo
Google AI Overview cites you: 1/9 keywords
Instead cites: monday.com (4x), asana.com (3x)
```

Also works with local models (Ollama/Qwen) — free, offline.
